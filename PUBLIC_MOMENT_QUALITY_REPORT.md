# Public moment quality & materialization

Generated by `npm run materialize:public-moments` (do not hand-edit counts; re-run the script).

## Quality rules (code)

- Reject ultra-generic **single-word** stems and a shared **GENERIC_MOMENT_TOKENS** blocklist (`lib/moments/public-moment-quality.ts`).
- Boost **"what is / what are"**, **"how to"**, **questions**, **digits**, **longer multi-word phrases**, **mixed-case hints**, and a small **tech lexicon** overlap.
- Require **snippet length** ≥ 88 chars and that the **phrase is reflected** in the merged snippet.
- **Strict** gate default: combined score ≥ **20**; if fewer than 50 moments pass, a **soft** pass (≥ **12**) fills toward the cap without re-scraping transcripts.
