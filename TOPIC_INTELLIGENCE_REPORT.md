## Internal linking strategy

- **Topic ↔ moment**: every hub surfaces canonical `/moment/[id]/[slug]` links for the strongest indexed excerpts.
- **Topic ↔ search**: primary label + related phrases deep-link to `/search/[query]` for continued exploration.
- **Topic ↔ video**: aggregate best videos by overlap count to `/video/[videoId]`.
- **Topic ↔ creator**: map channel names to `/creator/[slug]` where the creator database matches.
- **Topic ↔ topic**: overlap graph connects hubs that share videos (related topic chips).
- **Discovery**: `/topics` lists pillar-grouped hubs; sitemap adds **hub-only** intelligence URLs not already covered by curated `TOPIC_KEYWORDS`.

## Remaining risks

- **Label noise**: phrase-derived hubs can inherit long spoken fragments; tune grouping in `lib/topics/topic-index.ts`.
- **Overlap with curated topics**: seed pages keep canonical metadata; intelligence JSON-LD is emitted only for hub-quality pairings on seed URLs.
- **Stale index**: topic cache is in-memory per server process; redeploy or restart picks up new `public-moments.json`.
- **Thin pages**: `robots: noindex` reduces SEO noise while preserving crawl paths for internal discovery.
